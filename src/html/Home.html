Vote for Logic To change the political system, actively vote for nobody. By marking your ballot with "Logic not Politics" you cast a vote against politics. Your vote is recorded as a spoilt ballot, a vote against the political system. If and when Logic not Politics gains majority support, it signifies a collective demand for change.

Language Human language operates in two realms: the real and the abstract. Politics exploits abstract by creating an illogical system based on belief and opinion rather than reality. Politics in Disguise Economics is inherently political, not logical. Economics has no scientific foundation, prioritising power and profit over society and the ecosystem. A Sustainable Future To truly progress, we must reject politics and embrace science and logic. We must transform education to be scientific and logical, not political, enabling everyone to learn to enjoy life whilst nurturing the ecosystem. By voting for nobody, you help the migration towards a logical and sustainable society. We can return to enjoying nature, witnessing birdsong not traffic noise once again, as we did when economics was put on hold during the pandemic. In the 21st century, it is ridiculous that politically-whipped representatives vote in place of individuals. We can design 21st-century systems which ensure every voice is heard and carefully considered.

Note: Voting for nobody isn't about apathy; it's about actively rejecting a corrupt and illogical system and pushing for meaningful change.
